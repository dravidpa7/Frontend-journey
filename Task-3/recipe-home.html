Requirements

Category Item Quantity
Ingredients Idli rice (parboiled rice) 2 cups
Whole urad dal (black gram split & skinned) 1 cup
Fenugreek seeds (optional) ½ tsp
Water As needed
Salt As needed
Equipment Mixer or wet grinder 1
Idli steamer or pressure cooker (without weight) 1
Idli plates/moulds As needed
Large bowl for soaking 2
Spatula / spoon 1

Soaking

  1. Rinse 2 cups of idli rice and soak it in water for at least 6 hours.
  2. Rinse 1 cup of urad dal and soak it along with ½ tsp fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.

Grinding

  1. Grind urad dal + fenugreek in a wet grinder or blender using little water till you get a smooth, fluffy batter.
  2. Next, grind the rice with water until it's slightly coarse (like fine semolina).
  3. Mix both batters together in a large bowl.

Fermentation

  1. Add salt (around 1 tsp) and mix well.
  2. Cover and keep the batter in a warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight to ferment.
  3. The batter should double in volume and appear bubbly.

Steaming

  1. Grease the idli moulds with oil.
  2. Pour the fermented batter into the moulds.
  3. Steam in an idli steamer or pressure cooker (without the whistle) for 10–15 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Check by inserting a toothpick—it should come out clean.

"Serve hot with sambar and chutney (like coconut or tomato chutney)."
